How it works

Show up.
Play hard.
Win big.

The host runs everything from a laptop. Players join on their phones. The TV shows the action. The bar fills up.

01

Venue sets up

Open the host dashboard on any laptop. Connect it to the TV. You're live in under two minutes.

02

Players join

Scan a QR code or type a 6-digit PIN. Pick a team name. That's it.

03

Game begins

Questions hit the TV. Answers go through phones. The whole room plays at once, live.

04

Mini-games take over

Every 5 questions, the trivia pauses and something chaotic happens. Bets. Horses. Rockets. Roulette. Oh, and there's a 10-minute halftime — perfect time to grab another round.

🍺 Bar rush built in
05

Podium moment

Final leaderboard. Confetti. The winners get crowned — and walk away with free drinks, a prize, or whatever the venue puts up. Losers start planning their comeback.
